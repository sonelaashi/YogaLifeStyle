TEENAGERS DIET PLAN

Morning

Lemon water(Add 1 cup water + 1/2 Lemon + Black Salt)

Breakfast

Coasely ground wheat(Thuli) + ghee(Tadka) (1 big Bowl) or Oats or Bread Omelette(4 pisces of Bread). After 5-10 min than take milk + almonds(10)

Lunch

Any type of Dal You can take(1 Bowl) + 3 Chapati + Salad + Lassi(Without Sugar)

Evening Snacks

Any type of Fruit you can take or Fruit Salad.

Lunch

Any type of Sabji(2 Bowl) + 2 Chapati + Salad At Night 1 cup Milk.
